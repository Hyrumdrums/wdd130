The Ice Breaker

This wild trip will have you doing more than just rafting. At 6 points along the river you'll have to get out and navigate the river on foot. Sometimes we even need to break sections of ice to get our rafts through. Pack your ice picks!

Freezer Falls

Didn't think ice could be any cooler? Think again. Final destination, the culdesac of ice! This trip is perfect for those who want to experience the coldest part of the river.

Final Destination

Tired of life threatening temperatures? Seen it all? Take a step in the shoes of our guides, and experience what it's like to rescue our customers (which happens more than you'd think). All gear included as well as a free T-shirt!
